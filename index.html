About renovia

Built for
hard stories

renovia exists because aesthetic medicine has long focused on enhancement for its own sake. We believe the most profound work is restoration — giving back something that was taken. Tunisia's first holistic reconstruction and regenerative wellness centre.

"The body keeps the score. We believe it also holds the blueprint for who you were — and who you can become."

renovia was founded on a simple conviction: that aesthetic surgery, when done in service of restoration rather than vanity, is one of the most dignified things medicine can offer. It says: what happened to you matters. Your body matters. You deserve to feel like yourself again.

We pair that conviction with the best surgical talent in North Africa and the Mediterranean, and a regenerative retreat programme inspired by the world's most progressive wellness centres.
